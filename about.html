Meet your host

Hi, I'm Sarah.

I'm a formally trained artist from Kildare with a passion for making creativity feel accessible to absolutely everyone. I started Álainn because I genuinely believe that painting shouldn't be intimidating — it should be joyful, social and something you look forward to.

Whether you've never picked up a brush before or you paint regularly at home, my classes are designed to meet you exactly where you are. I'll guide you through every step — so you can relax, enjoy the process, and surprise yourself with what you create.

  • Every student leaves feeling proud of what they made
  • No experience needed — ever
  • Classes are relaxed, fun and always a proper night out
  • All materials provided — just bring yourself
