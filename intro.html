Welcome to Álainn

Painting classes made for you

Álainn is a series of creative painting classes hosted across Kildare by Sarah Mulvaney. Whether you're coming for a date night, a hen party, a catch-up with friends, or simply a little time for yourself — you don't need any experience, any talent, or any plan. Just show up, pick up a brush, and let the evening take care of the rest.

Every class is social, relaxed and genuinely memorable — the kind of night you'll still be talking about the week after.

Creative

No blank canvas anxiety here — Sarah guides you every step of the way, so everyone leaves with something they're genuinely proud of.

Social

Classes are designed to be shared — with your partner, your friends, your colleagues, or whoever you love spending time with.

Memorable

You'll go home with a painting you made yourself and memories of an evening that felt like more than just a night out.

"Laugh, learn, connect & get creative."
