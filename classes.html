Every occasion, one paintbrush

Our classes are open to everyone — whatever brings you through the door, you're in the right place.

for two

Date Night

Skip the usual dinner and do something you'll actually remember. Paint side by side, laugh at each other's masterpieces, and go home with something to hang on the wall.

for the squad

Hen Parties

A hen party with a twist — creative, chaotic (in the best way), and completely unforgettable. The perfect alternative to a night you've done a hundred times before.

for good company

Friend Catch-ups

More interesting than coffee, more relaxed than a night out. Catch up properly while you paint — and leave with a new story to tell.

for your team

Corporate Events

A team activity that people actually look forward to. Get your group away from their desks, into a creative headspace, and bonding over something completely new.

for just yourself

Weekly Classes

A regular slot that's just for you. Come solo, meet new people, and give yourself an hour or two each week that feels like a proper break.

Whatever the occasion — you're welcome here.
